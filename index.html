About

The idea for Mount came from the Gates Invention competition Madi competed in when she was twelve. She created a retractable bike lock that was built into her bicycle. She won the competetion and with it a fully funded patent.

Today, Mount is a micromobility intermediary. We work with city governments and scooter companies bringing them together through our locks + software solution. We bring order and regulation to an otherwise chaotic market.
